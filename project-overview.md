# InternTrail — Project Overview

**Tagline:** Follow the trail to your first internship

## Problem

Students struggle to find internships specifically for freshers. Existing job boards mix senior roles with internships, making it hard for new graduates and university students to find relevant opportunities.

## Target Users

- University students (any year)
- Fresh graduates entering the job market for the first time

## Goals

- Surface fresher-friendly internships clearly and quickly
- Avoid clutter from senior/experienced roles
- Keep the experience lightweight and fast — no login, no backend

## Tech Stack

- **HTML** — structure and semantic markup
- **CSS** — styling, layout, responsive design
- **JavaScript** — filtering, interactivity, hardcoded data

No backend, no frameworks, no build tools. Pure vanilla web stack.
